Privacy Policy (en-US)

Who am I and what is this Privacy Policy?

diegogiacomelli.com.br is a blog dedicated to C# programming and game development. The website is owned and operated by Diego Giacomelli. This Privacy Policy describes the ways in which your personal data is collected, stored, used and protected. You accept this policy and consent to such collection, storage and use by accessing my blog. This policy can be changed at any time by posting a revised version on the blog. The revised version will take effect as soon as it is made available online.

How is information about you collected?

When you visit the blog diegogiacomelli.com.br, Google Analytics, Google AdSense and Cloudflare collect your IP address and standard web access information such as your browser type and the pages you have accessed.

How are cookies used?

When you access diegogiacomelli.com.br, Google Analytics, Google AdSense and Cloudflare may place small data files called “cookies” on your computer. They send a “session cookie” to your computer when you access my blog. This type of cookie helps to recognize you when you revisit the blog and while you visit various pages on it.

How is personal information protected and stored?

The blog diegogiacomelli.com.br does not store any personal information about you directly, any form of collection or storage of information is done through third-party services, such as: Google Analytics, Google AdSense and Cloudflare

Additional Information

More details on how Google Analytics, Google AdSense and Cloudflare handle your data can be accessed at the links below:
  • Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=pt-BR
  • Cloudflare: https://www.cloudflare.com/en-us/privacypolicy

Como são protegidas e armazenadas informações pessoais?

O blog diegogiacomelli.com.br não armazena nenhum informação pessoal sua diretamente, qualquer forma de coleta ou armazenamento de informações é feita atráves de serviços terceiros, como: Google Analytics, Google AdSense e Cloudflare
